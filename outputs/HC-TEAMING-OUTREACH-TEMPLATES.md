## HOW TO FIND SBLO CONTACTS

1. **SAM.gov** → search for large prime contractors in your NAICS codes → look for awards >$750K → find the awardee company name
2. **LinkedIn** → search "[Company name] Small Business Liaison Officer" or "SBLO" or "Subcontracting"
3. **Company website** → most large primes have a "Supplier Diversity" or "Small Business" page with SBLO contact
4. **GSA eBuy** → primes registered as large businesses on GSA vehicles often list SBLO contacts

**Top targets to research first:**
- Booz Allen Hamilton (sblo contact via boozallen.com/suppliers)
- SAIC (smallbusiness@saic.com)
- CACI International (supplier diversity team)
- ManTech International
- DXC Technology
- Perspecta (now SAIC)
- Leidos Holdings
- Any mid-size federal integrator in logistics/environmental space

## WHAT MAKES A GOOD TEAMING AGREEMENT

When a prime responds, here's what to negotiate:

- **Scope:** Specific deliverables H&C performs (not vague "support")
- **Value:** Minimum 10% of prime contract value (aim for 15–25%)
- **Exclusivity clause:** They cannot bring in another SDVOSB for this opportunity
- **Flow-down clauses:** Standard — accept them (FAR requirements)
- **Payment terms:** Net 30 from prime's receipt of government payment
- **Performance period:** Tied to base + all option years
- **Termination for convenience:** Standard — make sure it's mutual
