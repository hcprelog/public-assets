## GSA MULTIPLE AWARD SCHEDULE — HIGH-PRIORITY PURSUIT

**This is the single most important strategic move H&C can make in 2026.**

A GSA MAS contract puts H&C on the federal government's preferred vendor list. Agencies can buy directly from MAS vendors without going through full competition. For H&C, this means:

- Contracting Officers can award to H&C without a competitive solicitation
- H&C appears in GSA Advantage! (searched by federal buyers daily)
- No SDVOSB/HUBZone set-aside needed — direct award
- Dramatically reduces per-contract business development cost

**Target SINs (Schedule Input Numbers):**
- SIN 541614 — Logistics Consulting
- SIN 562910 — Environmental/Remediation Services

**Application requirements:**
- 2 years in business ✅ (H&C registered 2023)
- Past performance (at least 1 reference) ✅ (VA contract)
- Financial statements (2 years)
- Pricing proposal
- Approved pricelist

**Timeline:** 3–6 months from application to award. Start NOW.

**Action:** Go to gsaelibrary.gsa.gov → Find your SIN → Download solicitation → Complete SAM.gov registration update first.
